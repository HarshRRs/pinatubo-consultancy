How we protect and use your information

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Information We Collect

Pinatubo Consultancy collects personal information necessary to provide our administrative and legal services, including:

  • Full name, contact details (email, phone number)
  • Identity documents for visa and administrative applications
  • Financial information for business creation services
  • Other information you provide during consultations

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide administrative and legal assistance services
  • Communicate with you about your case or appointment
  • Submit applications to French authorities on your behalf
  • Comply with legal obligations under French law

3. Data Protection (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at pinatubofr@gmail.com.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We only share your information with:

  • French government authorities (prefectures, CPAM, CAF, etc.) as required for your applications
  • Professional partners (certified accountants, translators) with your consent

6. Cookies

This website may use essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics without your consent.
