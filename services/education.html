Education & Students

Navigate your academic journey in France with expert guidance

Your Challenge

Studying in France is an incredible opportunity, but the administrative process can be overwhelming. From Campus France registration to student visa applications, Parcoursup submissions, and finding suitable housing—each step requires specific documentation and precise procedures.

Common obstacles students face:

  • Complex Campus France procedures
  • Student visa (VLS-TS) application confusion
  • Parcoursup platform navigation
  • Finding affordable student housing (CROUS or private)
  • Carte de séjour renewals and status changes

Our Solution

We've helped hundreds of international students successfully navigate the French education system. Our team handles the bureaucracy so you can focus on your studies.

We ensure:

  • ✓ Accurate document preparation
  • ✓ Timely submissions
  • ✓ Follow-ups with authorities
  • ✓ Housing search assistance
  • ✓ CAF application support

What We Offer

Student Visa Assistance

Complete support for VLS-TS (long-stay student visa) applications, including document verification, form completion, and appointment scheduling.

Campus France Registration

Guidance through the Campus France platform, from account creation to interview preparation and document submission.

Parcoursup Guidance

Expert help navigating Parcoursup for university admissions, including application strategy and letter of motivation review.

Student Housing

Assistance finding accommodation through CROUS or private rentals, including lease review and move-in support.

CAF Student Aid

Complete CAF application support to help you receive housing assistance (APL/ALS) you're entitled to.

Carte de Séjour Renewal

Timely renewal assistance for your student residence permit, including status change applications when needed.
