Social Assistance

Access your rights and benefits in the French social system

Your Challenge

France has a robust social security system, but accessing it can be a bureaucratic nightmare. Multiple agencies (CAF, CPAM, France Travail) operate independently, each with its own forms, portals, and requirements. Language barriers often lead to unclaimed benefits.

Common difficulties:

  • Obtaining a Carte Vitale (Health Insurance card)
  • Applying for housing aid (APL) with CAF
  • Registering for unemployment benefits
  • Understanding retirement pension rights
  • Navigating disability support (MDPH)

Our Solution

We serve as your guide and intermediary with French social agencies. We ensure you understand your rights and help you submit complete, accurate applications to receive the benefits you are entitled to.

We ensure:

  • ✓ Rights assessment
  • ✓ Form completion & submission
  • ✓ Account creation (Ameli, CAF, etc.)
  • ✓ Document translation coordination
  • ✓ Dispute resolution with agencies

What We Offer

Health Insurance (Ameli)

Assistance with registering for French social security, obtaining your Carte Vitale, and setting up a mutuelle (supplemental insurance).

CAF Housing Aid

Complete support for APL (Aide Personnalisée au Logement) applications and Prime d'Activité declarations.

Unemployment (France Travail)

Help with registration at Pôle Emploi (now France Travail), monthly updates, and understanding your benefit calculations.

Disability Rights (MDPH)

Guidance for AAH (Allocation Adulte Handicapé) applications and recognition of worker disability status (RQTH).

Retirement Pension

Assistance with preparing your retirement file, calculating points, and applying for your French pension.

Transport & Solidarity

Help applying for reduced transport fares (Navigo Solidarité) and other local social benefits.
