About Pinatubo Consultancy

Your trusted partner for administrative and legal guidance in France

Our Story

Pinatubo Consultancy was founded with a simple mission: to help individuals and businesses navigate the complex administrative and legal landscape of France. We understand firsthand the challenges that come with relocating to a new country, starting a business, or managing legal documentation.

Located in the heart of Paris at 98 Boulevard du Montparnasse, we have been serving students, entrepreneurs, and residents for years, providing expert guidance with a personal touch.

Our founder, Lira Mae de Demo, recognized the need for multilingual, compassionate administrative support and built a consultancy that puts clients first.
